About Brand of Brown

Brand of Brown is your trusted destination for authentic pre-owned luxury handbags. We specialize in curating a premium selection of designer bags from the world's most prestigious brands including Chanel, Gucci, YSL, Balenciaga, Prada, BOYY, and Celine.

With years of experience in the luxury goods market, we pride ourselves on offering only genuine, authenticated items to our discerning clientele. Each piece in our collection is carefully inspected and verified to ensure its authenticity and quality.

Whether you're looking to buy your dream handbag, sell a piece from your collection, or explore consignment options, Brand of Brown provides a seamless and trustworthy experience. We also offer flexible installment payment plans to make luxury more accessible.

Our Mission

To provide a trusted platform for luxury handbag enthusiasts to buy, sell, and consign authentic pre-owned designer bags. We are committed to transparency, authenticity, and exceptional customer service in every transaction.
